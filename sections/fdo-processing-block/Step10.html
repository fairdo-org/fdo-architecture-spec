Description

This step consists in accessing the FDO’s Data.

Inputs

The Validated_Profiles with their respective attributes from step 8.

Outputs

There are four different possible outputs:

  • The in-line data value
  • An FDO PID
  • An FDO Profile
  • A URI
  • A null value.
Expected behavior

The Client inspects each of the Validated_Profiles and finds their respective 0.FDO/Data_Ref attributes to find the data attributes. The client should make a best effort to provide the means to access the data depending on the manner in which the FDO references its data.
